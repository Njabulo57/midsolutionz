Mamqoco Innovative Design Solutions (MID) is vibrant and innovative Architecture and Construction firm-based Johannesburg, Gauteng, with reach across the country. We are a youthful, diverse, energetic company with alternative design methodologies, innovative ideas, a client centred approach and a creative skill in use of materiality, to come to exciting solutions to your everyday problem.

About Us

We apply a fresh and innovative approach to Architecture, reflecting our commitment to excellent design, structural integrity, and conscious stewardship of the built environment. We focus and direct our energies to ensure all our projects are client orientated, energy efficient, climate and site-specific by using creative design methodologies.

We as Mamqoco Innovative Design Solutions value our clients' needs over everything.

Our focus is to ensure that all our clients concerns, issues and problems are resolved with the utmost care and dignity, we prefer to have our clients with us every step of the way.

Allowing us to exercise design freedom while meeting our clients brief within time and budget. We specialise in low to high dense residential designs, commercial office blocks, clinics, community centres, community-based development designs and entertainment hubs for social inclusive strategies and design.

The company offers services in Architecture, Interior Design, Engineering, Town Planning, Land surveying and Construction, where depending on the scope of work we provide, 3D visualizations, animations and marketing videos.

We run a full-service company, whatever you need, we will execute it accordingly.

We Always take into consideration that clients are specific in their needs.

Services We Offer

Architecture

Mamqoco Innovative Design Solutions offers Architectural services from Stage 1 - Inception, through to Stage 6 - Close Out.
We take our clients on a journey through the design process, giving them leeway to watch their designs become reality, providing them with 3 Sketch Plan Designs, 1 Detailed Design, 1 Finalised design to be submitted to the municipality and where applicable, Construction Working Drawings.

Construction

Mamqoco Innovative Design Solutions offers Full construction services, inclusive of Engineering Services. We submit building plans to NHBRC and facilitate the entire construction process, specific to your design. We construct your design in 6 phases, Foundation, Brickwork and Framing , Openings and Detail, Superstructure and Detail, Plastering and Finishes, and Hand Over.

Interior Design

Mamqoco Innovative Design Solutions offers Full Interior Design services. We create a mood board for the exact interior finish a client looks for and compile a detailed finishes schedule once a design is in place. Making the process more tangible we offer realistic imagery for our clients and take trips to stores with our clients to give them full exposure the final product that is their design and where applicable we customize and redesign.
